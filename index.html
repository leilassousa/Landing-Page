Since 2019, the Penalty S11 Ecoknit Ball has been used in Campeonato Paulista, reusing 4.5 PET bottles per unit. Made with 62% sustainable or recyclable materials, including sugarcane derivatives, this FIFA-certified ball has helped recycle over 500,000 bottles—a number projected to exceed 250,000 by the end of 2024.
